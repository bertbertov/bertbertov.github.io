Manga Offline

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026 · Developer: Albert Kamalov · Contact: bertbertov@gmail.com

Summary

Manga Offline collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no ads, no analytics, no telemetry.

What data Manga Offline handles

  • The list of comic-book archives (.cbz / .zip files) you choose to open, plus your reading progress per book, is stored locally.
  • The app reads only the files you explicitly pick via the Android Storage Access Framework. It never browses your storage on its own.
  • No file content is uploaded.

Permissions used

Manga Offline requests no runtime permissions. File access uses the Storage Access Framework: Android shows you a system file-picker dialog, and the app receives access only to the specific file you tap.

What we never collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any other identifier.
  • Your location (unless explicitly noted in “Permissions used” above).
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or anything that could uniquely identify you.
  • Crash reports, usage analytics, behavioral telemetry — none of it.

Third parties

Manga Offline contains no third-party SDKs that transmit data. There are no analytics providers, no ad networks, no crash reporters, no remote configuration services.

Children

Manga Offline is not directed at children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data, no special protections under COPPA / GDPR-K are required, but you should still use your own judgment about whether this app is appropriate for the child in your care.

Your data, your control

All data Manga Offline stores lives in a local database on your device. To delete everything: uninstall the app. Android removes the local database when the app is uninstalled.

If you have Google Drive automatic backup enabled in Android Settings, an encrypted copy of the app’s local data may be included in your system-level Google backup. That backup is controlled by Google’s privacy policy, not by Manga Offline, and is restored only if you reinstall on a new device while signed in to the same Google account.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change. Material changes (e.g. if a future version adds optional cloud sync) will be disclosed in the app’s release notes on Google Play before the changed version ships.
